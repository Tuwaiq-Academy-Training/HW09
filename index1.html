French Onion Soup

French Onion Soup


We have trying French onion in restaurant for years and my family and friends agree none can compare to my recipe for taste and simpilicity of preparation.



Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  2. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  3. 4 cups sliced onions
  4. 4 (10.5 ounce) cans beef broth
  5. 2 tablespoons dry sherry (optiona)
  6. 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  7. salt and pepper to taste
  8. 4 slices French bread
  9. 4 slices provolone cheese
  10. 2 slices Swiss cheese, diced
  11. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    Direction

    • Melt butter with olive oil in an 8 quart stock pot on medium heat. Add onions and continually stir until tender and translucent. Do not brown the onions.
    • Add beef broth, sherry and thyme. Season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 30 minutes
    • Heat the oven broiler.
    • Ladle soup into oven safe serving bows and place one slice of bread on top of each (bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer). Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolone, 1/2 slice diced Swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Place bowls on cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven unsi cheese bubbles and browns slightly
